Fuel Prices in South Africa

Fuel prices in South Africa are set to increase from December 2024. Petrol prices will rise by 17 cents per litre, and diesel will increase by up to 56 cents per litre. These changes are attributed to the weakening rand and seasonal demand.

The price hike is partly due to the depreciation of the rand against the dollar and fluctuations in international oil prices. Despite global crude oil prices showing a slight decrease, the domestic impact is substantial due to the weaker currency.

Diesel prices have seen a sharp rise, largely driven by increased seasonal demand, with further implications for transport costs and logistics industries.
